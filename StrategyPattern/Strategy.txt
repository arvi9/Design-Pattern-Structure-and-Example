Whay is Strategy?
A strategy is a plan of action designed to achieve a specific goal.

What is Strategy Pattern?
Define a family of algorithms, encapsulate each one and make them interchangable. Strategy lets the algorithm vary independently from clients that use it.
-------------------------------------------------------------------
Example:

Problem for Shopping Mall
----------------
1. Accept customer details
2. Calculate bill amount
3. Apply discount based on day of week
	1. Monday - Low discount - 10%
	2. Thursday - High Discount - 50%

Note:
-----------------
Shopping Mall encapsulate three thing
1. Customer Details
2. Calculate Bill after applying discount
3. Get Final Bill
